Is dark mode truly better?

September 16, 2021 by Monique Kellman

There's no doubt that the aesthetics of dark mode are truly amazing. As a programmer, it's prolly sacriligious of me to even pose this question but seriously... What's so wrong with the basic white background? Does the dark theme really help with our eye-sight or is it just all hype? Have you ever tried to read your whatsapp messages while on the road in broad daylight? Your phone is at max brightness and yet still you gotta cover it a little to attempt to make out the words. Now having the regular light theme would prolly have some level of difficulty seeing as well but ... hear me out... it could be a tad bit better experience....

That place where you're focused on the task at hand and there's something to motivate you to push through all those compiler and runtime errors. Let's talk genres. Personally, soca is my number 1 but I know my audience is more inclined to... sigh ... dancehall. We're not gonna go into the soca vs dancehall war today but you know cough, cough... soca for life.
